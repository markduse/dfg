Final Expense

Mortgage Protection
Ensures your family can keep the home if you pass away or become disabled — one of the most important protections a homeowner can carry.
  • Covers your mortgage balance
  • Disability & critical illness riders
  • Return of premium options
  • Linked to your loan payoff

Indexed Universal Life
A permanent life policy with cash value tied to market performance — protect your family and build tax-advantaged wealth simultaneously.
  • Tax-free wealth accumulation
  • Market-linked growth with floor
  • Flexible premiums
  • Retirement income supplement
⏱️
Term Life Insurance
Pure, affordable coverage for a set period. The most cost-effective way to protect your family during your highest-earning years.
  • 10, 20, or 30-year terms
  • Coverage up to $2M+
  • Convertible to permanent
  • Lowest premiums available
♾️
Whole Life Insurance
Guaranteed lifetime coverage with a cash value component that grows over time. Simple, reliable, and built to last.
  • Lifetime coverage guaranteed
  • Cash value grows tax-deferred
  • Fixed premiums for life
  • Dividends with participating carriers

Fixed Indexed Annuities
Guaranteed income for life with market-linked upside and zero downside risk. The ultimate retirement security tool for conservative investors.
  • Guaranteed income for life
  • Principal protection
  • Tax-deferred growth
  • Inflation-adjusted income riders

Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

What types of people does DFG help? +
We serve families looking for life insurance coverage — final expense, mortgage protection, term, whole life, IUL, and annuities. Every client gets the same commitment to quality guidance and fast results.
How much does it cost to get a quote? +
Absolutely nothing. Getting a quote with DFG is 100% free. We shop your profile across 25+ carriers to find the best rate for your age, health, and coverage needs. No obligation, no pressure, no hidden fees.
Do I need a medical exam to get coverage? +
For most of our popular products — especially Final Expense and Mortgage Protection — no medical exam is required. Approval is based on a few health questions and can happen in minutes. We'll guide you through the easiest path.
How fast can I get covered? +
Same-day and next-day coverage is available on most of our final expense and mortgage protection products. Many clients receive their policy documents within 24–48 hours of their application.
I have a pre-existing condition. Can I still get covered? +
Yes — and this is exactly where DFG shines. We work with 25+ carriers, including specialists in coverage for clients with diabetes, heart conditions, COPD, cancer history, and many other conditions. Don't assume you're uninsurable before talking to us.
What states does DFG operate in? +
We are licensed and actively writing business in all 50 states. Our agents operate 100% remotely, so no matter where you or your clients are located, we can help.
