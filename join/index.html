Questions

Common questions.

Do I need a license to start?+
This is a licensed financial services industry. If you're already licensed — great, let's talk. If not, we have a structured 14-day study program that gets you licensed and dialing fast.
Is this commission-only?+
Yes. We're building partners, not employees. The average W2 job pays $45K/year. Our producers target $10K+/month. We give you the full system — you provide the work ethic and we split the upside.
How do the leads work?+
We own our own lead generation operation — Pinnacle Lead Shop. Every lead comes from a real person who went online, saw our ads, and requested life insurance information. You're speaking to interested buyers, not cold-calling strangers.
What does a typical day look like?+
Log in, load your CRM, dial your leads using our proven script, run quotes in real time, submit applications. Top agents work 5–6 focused hours and generate more than most people do in a full 40-hour week.
Can I do this part-time?+
We strongly prefer full-time commitment while you're building momentum. We do accept serious part-time agents who can commit 20+ hours per week, but results reflect the hours you put in.
What makes DFG different from other IMOs?+
Most agencies give you a contract and wish you luck. We built the lead shop, wrote the playbook, mapped out the 10-year income path, and we're on the phone with agents daily. The infrastructure we run would cost you $50K+ to replicate on your own.
